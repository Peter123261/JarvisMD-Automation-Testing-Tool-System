#### Assessment

#### Plan
  * Recommend keeping a headache diary to identify potential triggers.
  * Prescribe strong painkillers such as paracetamol or cocodamol, and anti-inflammatories like ibuprofen or naproxen.
  * Consider prophylactic medications if headaches become more persistent.
  * Advise the patient to return if symptoms do not improve in 2-3 days or if they worsen, particularly if there is increased headache severity, worsening neck pain, weakness in arms or legs, or vision changes.
